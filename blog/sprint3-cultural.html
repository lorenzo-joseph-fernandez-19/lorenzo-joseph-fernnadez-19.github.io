Emotional Intelligence

5 December 2019

"What is Emotional Intelligence?"

Emotional Intelligence is about being aware of your actions and your feelings and how your actions and feelings affect those around you.
It's also about being mindful of how others are feeling and responding appropriately.

"How is it different to IQ?"

Emotional Intelligence differs from IQ in that it:

  1. Assesses the emotions of a person, of others and of groups (while IQ assess the intelligence of an individual).
  2. It can be developed and improved whilst IQ cannot.
  3. Ensures success in life whilst IQ ensures success in school.
  4. Recognises leaders, captains, managers and people with social challenges while IQ recognises people with high intellect, common sense as well as mental challenges.

"Why is it important?"

Emotional Intelligence is important as it can lead a person on a path towards a more happy and fulfilled life, support the advancement towards academic and professional success, improve relationships as well as boost communication skills.
Emotional Intelligence can also help a person better manage stress, form and develop meaningful human relationships, as well as improve their physical and psychological health.
