My Bootcamp Learning Plan

"What is your long term goal and/or career pathway?"

To be an entrepreneur and run my own business and to be able to work from home if not remotely.

"What do you think your biggest strengths and limitations will be in Bootcamp?"

I think my people skills would be one of my biggest strengths throughout Bootcamp as it would help me with pair programming as well as help me stay sane. Furthermore I believe my biggest limitations would be around not asking for help as well as not prioritising rest and breaks throughout Bootcamp.

"What do you think your biggest non-technical challenge at Bootcamp will be?

I feel that my biggest non-technical challenge would be staying focussed for 9 weeks as well as prioritising rest and breaks.

"What non-technical skills - human skills - would you like to see developed in yourself while at EDA?"

During Bootcamp I would like to develop my self awareness and reflective skills as this is something that I want to do more of.

"What are you expectations from the Bootcamp team?"

Fundamentally I expect the Bootcamp team to be understanding and supportive especially when I'm struggling to follow or understand the learning material.

"What are your expectations of yourself at Bootcamp?"

  1. That i will stay on top of the learning material and put in more work if I feel like I'm falling behind.
  2. That I will not refrain from asking for help but will do so even when I feel like I have a rough idea of the answer.
  3. That I will use my time wisely and space out time for work and time for rest/breaks.
  4. That I won't beat myself up when I don't understand something.
